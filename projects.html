Projects

Photogram

Photogram is a web application designed for anonymous photo sharing, akin to other social media platforms. Initially developed using the Django web framework, I later transformed it into a REST API-based system. The final iteration boasts a Vue.js frontend and Flask backend, all seamlessly integrated within a Docker environment.

Technologies: Django, Vue.js, Flask, PostgreSQL, Docker.

BalkanJob

BalkanJob is the platform connecting job seekers with companies offering employment opportunities. Users seeking employment can enhance their profiles to make a better impression on potential employers. Meanwhile, companies can easily share their job descriptions on the platform.

Technologies: Django, HTML, CSS, Vanilla JS, SQLite.

OSman Bot

OSman is the instagram bot that shows you the urls of users who do not following you back. Coded with Selenium module in Python.

Technologies: Python.

Mesh Designer

Mesh Designer is a web application utilized for generating patterns from uploaded images.
